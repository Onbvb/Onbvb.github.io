Information (And relevant spoilers!)

Pokemon Mystery Dungeon: Seikosis is... odd.
It began with Seiko, and his world.
His world being an urban fantasy setting that I've developed on-and-off for a while.
(Fun fact, he started life as a minecraft skin, as most of my original characters have.)
I love him, and his world, but back to PMD:S
It takes themes and ideas from the real PMD games, but it doesn't end up following any of them beat-for-beat, unlike many other (great) fan works.
I've stolen other ideas that break tradition from a really wonderful comic called PMD: On Borrowed Time.
It really... kicked me, mentally, into writing PMD:S. Go read it. It's great.
Most of the characters have their own tidbits about how they came into being, but that's on it's own page.
